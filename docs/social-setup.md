# Social Media Setup for TheSynLab

## Facebook Business Page
1. Go to facebook.com/business and click "Create Page"
2. Category: "Science, Technology & Engineering"
3. Name: "TheSynLab"
4. Bio: "Data-driven smart home & productivity reviews. Trust Scores help you make confident tech decisions."
5. Profile picture: TheSynLab logo (400x400px, PNG)
6. Cover photo: 1640x624px with tagline "Reviews You Can Trust"
7. Add website: https://thesynlab.com
8. Verify page

## Instagram Business Profile
1. Create or convert to Business profile
2. Username: @thesynlab (check availability)
3. Bio: "Smart home & productivity reviews with Trust Scores 🏠💻 Data-driven. No fluff."
4. Link in bio: https://thesynlab.com
5. Use Linktree or similar for multi-link bio

## Connect Both
- In Facebook Page settings → Instagram → Connect account
- Enable cross-posting

## Add to Footer
The footer already has social links. Add the Facebook and Instagram URLs:
- Facebook: https://facebook.com/thesynlab
- Instagram: https://instagram.com/thesynlab

## OG Image Guidelines
For social shares, use:
- 1200x630px
- TheSynLab logo top-left
- Bold headline
- Clean background (gradient or white)
- No more than 20% text area
